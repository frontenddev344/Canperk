Contact Us

We at CanPerks are committed to providing you with exceptional travel experiences across Canada. Should you have any inquiries, suggestions, or need assistance with your travel plans, please don't hesitate to get in touch with us. Our dedicated team is here to ensure your journey is smooth and memorable. Customer Support: For any questions related to your bookings, itineraries, or general travel assistance, our customer support team is available 24/7. You can reach us by email at support@canperks.ca.

Partnerships and Collaborations:

We love collaborating with like-minded businesses and travel enthusiasts. If you're interested in partnering with CanPerks or have any business inquiries, please email us at partnerships@canperks.ca.

Media and Press: For media inquiries or press-related matters, please contact our communications team at media@canperks.ca.

Feedback and Suggestions: Your feedback is invaluable to us as we continually strive to improve our services. If you have any suggestions or comments, please share them with us at feedback@canperks.ca.

Careers: Interested in joining the CanPerks team? Check our Careers page on the website for current job openings or send your resume to careers@canperks.ca

We look forward to hearing from you and being a part of your unforgettable Canadian adventures!

Safe travels, The CanPerks Team
